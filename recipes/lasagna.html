Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with made from scratch tomato sauce and delicious,
cheesy filling. I have found none better anywhere.
Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread, if desired.

    INGRIDIENTS
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
